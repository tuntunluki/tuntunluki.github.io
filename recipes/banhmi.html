Banh Mi Viet Nam

Banh Mi - Vietnamese Baguette, one of the most famous Vietnamese dishes on around the world.

Ingredient:

  • Fresh Baguette - or Banh Mi bread
  • Pork/Chicken/Beef meat/
  • Ham
  • Vietnamese Ham
  • Pate
  • mayonnaise
  • Pickled Carrot, Daikon Radish
  • cucumber/li>
  • Fresh herbs: cilantro, mint, basil,...

Steps:

  1. Split the Baguette down the centre to the top
  2. Spread a spoon of pate on one side, then mayonnaise on top
  3. Layer in the Meat, hams, Vietnamese hams, cucumber slice and green onion
  4. Stuff in plenty of carrots, daikon and coriander sprigs
  5. Sprinkle with fresh chili - as much as you dare!
  6. Drizzle with special sauce
  7. Close the Baguette and devour!
